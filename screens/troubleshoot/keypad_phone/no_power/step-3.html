Try External Supply !

Your battery has enough voltage to power on, but your phone is still not turning on. Try to use the external power supply to power it on. Guidance for using the external power supply is located in the right corner.

Select one of following after inspection

Steps for use power supply

1. Turn On the Power Supply using power switch.

2. Adjust voltage to 3.7V using voltage adjustment knobs.

3. Adjust current to range between 1A and 2A using current adjustment knobs.

4. Connect the positive lead from the power supply to the positive terminal on the phone.

5. Connect the negative lead from the power supply to the negative terminal on the phone.

6. Now, Attempt to power on the phone using its power button.
